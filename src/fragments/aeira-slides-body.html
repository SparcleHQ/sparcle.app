The Challenge

Enterprise search is broken by design

Knowledge is trapped in silos, search tools ignore access controls, and every scale upgrade means a costly replatform.

The Governance Gap Most enterprise search products expose data to users who shouldn't see it. Without ACL enforcement at the index level, every search result is a potential compliance violation.
Search results that ignore permissions

Employees see documents they shouldn't access. Confidential HR data, board materials, and financial reports leak through ungoverned search.

Every upgrade means a new integration

Moving from basic search to enterprise scale typically requires a full replatform: new APIs, new auth models, new vendor.

Knowledge scattered with no unified schema

Wikis, runbooks, KB articles, HR docs, each in a different format. No single index, no single query surface.

Scale surprises: usage spikes → unexpected costs or downtime

Without rate limiting, quotas, and clear tier boundaries, production search becomes unpredictable and expensive.

Patent-pending 4-axis ACL + verifiable audit chain

Aeira indexes ACL as a first-class dimension. Every doc carries country, office, role-band, and employment-type restrictions that are evaluated at query time. Patent-pending architecture. Every access decision is emitted to a sealed Merkle audit chain your auditor can verify without our cooperation.

4-axis ACL · patent-pending

Restrictions indexed at ingest. Documents not matching the requester's identity never enter the candidate set.

country office role-band employment-type

Plus 4 access classes: Public · Department · Group · User

Standalone audit verifier

Sealed Merkle chain, Ed25519-signed, KMS-rooted. Your auditor runs the verifier binary themselves, no Sparcle cooperation required.

JWT native

Every request validated via JWT. Identity claims pass through from your IdP (Okta, Entra, OneLogin, Auth0, Google, Zoho, or any compliant OIDC / SAML 2.0).

Per-tenant CMK

Vault today; AWS KMS, Azure Key Vault, and PKCS#11 HSM on the roadmap. Crypto-shred for HIPAA-aligned / GDPR Article 17 erasure. Deleting the key destroys the derived data.

Per-region keyrings

Enhanced+ tiers add region-bound key residency. EU data can't be read from a US replica, even by a tenant admin.

Namespace isolation

Each tenant gets a dedicated namespace with independent access boundaries. Tenant_id mandatory in every storage call, no implicit-default path.
