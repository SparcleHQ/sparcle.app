Questions?

What do BYOK and BYO LLM mean?

BYOK (Bring Your Own Key) — plug in API keys from OpenAI, Anthropic, Google, Azure, or any compatible provider. BYO LLM (Bring Your Own LLM) — run self-hosted or private models in your own environment. Bolt supports both. You control the model, the cost, and the data flow. Available on all plans. Bundled and Complete also include managed AI if you prefer zero setup.

How does Bolt connect to our tools?

Bolt uses the open Model Context Protocol (MCP) to connect to your SaaS tools — Slack, Jira, Notion, Salesforce, and more. No data migration needed.

What happens if we exceed the AI query limit?

You'll never be cut off. Overages are billed at a transparent per-query rate. We'll alert you before you approach limits. Contact us for details on fair-use thresholds and custom allowances.

Is there a pilot program?

Yes — we offer a 30-day production-grade pilot. Contact us for details on pilot pricing and how credits are applied toward your first annual contract.

Where does our data live?

With Absolute — entirely in your environment. With Bundled — your infra + Bolt's AI gateway (covered by DPA, zero-retention). With Complete — Bolt-managed infrastructure with your choice of region (US, EU, APAC).

Can we switch plans later?

Yes. Start with Absolute (BYOK / BYO LLM) and upgrade to Bundled or Complete anytime. Your data, integrations, and configuration carry over seamlessly.

Questions?

Is Aeira Catalog really included?

Yes. Catalog is included in every Bolt plan so teams can start with governed enterprise discovery immediately. You only pay extra when you need Dynamic, Enhanced, or Federated scale profiles.

Will our API integrations break when upgrading tiers?

No. Aeira uses a consistent API contract and ACL model across tiers. Upgrades increase throughput, governance depth, and operational capabilities without forcing integration rewrites.

Can we use Aeira without the Bolt platform?

Aeira is bundled with the Bolt platform. Catalog is included with every Bolt plan. Paid Data tiers (Dynamic, Enhanced, Federated) are add-ons to any Bolt plan.

How does Aeira handle access control?

JWT-based ACL filtering is built into every tier. Your existing identity provider controls who sees what — the same ACL semantics apply whether you're on Catalog or Federated.

What's the difference between self-hosted and Sparcle-hosted?

Self-hosted means the data plane runs in your infrastructure — you control storage, compute, and network. Sparcle-hosted means we manage it for you with multi-region options and SLA guarantees.

How does pricing scale with active users?

Paid tiers are billed per active user per month. An active user is anyone who queries or writes to the data plane in a given billing period. Inactive users don't count.
