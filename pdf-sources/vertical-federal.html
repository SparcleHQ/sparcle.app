CUI + NIST 800-171

Sparcle: The 4-axis ACL with country · office · role-band · employment-type maps to the 800-171 access-control family. Documents pre-filter at index time; access decisions audit-log in a tamper-evident Merkle chain. CMMC-aware contractors adopt the same architecture in their enclave.

FIPS 140 cryptography

Your FIPS posture covers the crypto path; Sparcle is the orchestration layer.
