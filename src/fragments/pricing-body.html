FAQ

Questions?

What do BYOK and BYO LLM mean?

BYOK (Bring Your Own Key): plug in API keys from your existing enterprise agreements with Azure OpenAI, AWS Bedrock, Google, or Anthropic. BYO LLM (Bring Your Own LLM): run self-hosted or private models in your own environment. Bolt supports both. This means zero unpredictable token markups. You can securely burn down your existing cloud spend commitments. With a BYO self-hosted LLM, nothing leaves your perimeter at all; with BYOK to a cloud provider, only PII-masked prompts reach the endpoint you control.

How does Bolt connect to our tools?

Bolt uses the open Model Context Protocol (MCP) to connect to your SaaS tools: Slack, Jira, Notion, Salesforce, and more. No data migration needed.

Do you mark up AI tokens?

No. BYO-LLM is the default in every tier. You connect your own provider (OpenAI, Anthropic, Bedrock, Azure, or a local model) and pay your provider directly at your own rate. We never mark up tokens. There is no Sparcle inference gateway and no Sparcle-managed key option. Your provider bills you directly, so there are no surprise bills from us.

Is there a free option?

Bolt is free for individuals: download Bolt and get the full desktop suite with optional BYO IDP, email, calendar, and agentic actions. No credit card required, no data leaves your machine. For organizations, the paid tiers add SSO, central admin, policy, and audit: all self-hosted in your own infrastructure. Teams with IT/SRE can self-deploy free with our Helm charts and guides; optional one-time deployment services (Guided Setup at $5K or Production Deployment at $15K) are available if you want us to deploy and integrate with you.

Where does our data live?

Entirely in your own infrastructure, in every tier. Authorize, Backbone, and Certify all self-host inside your perimeter. Sparcle never hosts your data and never runs your inference. You bring your own LLM under your own provider contract, so no prompt ever transits Sparcle infrastructure.

Can we switch plans later?

Yes. Start with Authorize (BYO-LLM) and upgrade to Backbone or Certify anytime. Your data, integrations, and configuration carry over seamlessly, and you stay self-hosted throughout.

What do we need to deploy Bolt?

Every tier self-hosts: deploy via Docker Compose (single VM) or Kubernetes (HA) in your own infrastructure. You’ll need a Redis instance and your identity provider configured. We provide deployment guides and Helm charts so teams with IT/SRE can self-deploy free. Certify adds a dedicated delivery engineer who works with your IT/SRE on deployment, upgrades, and air-gap setup.

How long does deployment take?

Docker Compose staging setups typically run in 1–2 hours. Production Kubernetes deployments with SSO, networking, and compliance review typically take 1–2 weeks with our guidance. We offer optional deployment engineering services for teams that want a turnkey handoff.

FAQ

Questions?

Is Aeira Catalog really free?

Yes. Catalog is a curated company directory included with every Bolt plan: 8 core categories plus 2 custom, 50 records each. It helps employees find the right office, policy, app, or contact on day one. You only pay when you need the full search platform (Dynamic+).

What’s the difference between Catalog and Dynamic?

Catalog is a curated directory with keyword search and manual updates. Think of it as your company’s quick-reference index. Dynamic is a full enterprise search platform: ingest any document or data, search with hybrid AI (keyword + semantic vector), use 20 ready-made entity templates or define your own via API, and enforce 4-level ACL per user.

Can I index any type of corporate data?

Yes. On Dynamic and above, you can ingest any document: wiki pages, runbooks, onboarding guides, knowledge base articles, internal docs, and more. The 20 built-in entity templates are a starting point for common HR/IT/ops data, not a limit. Use the API to define and ingest your own types.

Do you offer a Sparcle-hosted option?

No, and that is deliberate. There is no Sparcle-hosted tier of Bolt or Aeira, so Sparcle never becomes a sub-processor on your register, never triggers a third-country transfer assessment, and never holds a key to your data. Every tier self-hosts inside your perimeter. What we do offer is deployment engineering, architecture and migration planning, custom SLA tiers, and ongoing support. We help you run it; we don't run it for you.

What search technology powers Aeira?

Paid tiers use hybrid search combining Postgres full-text search (BM25 keyword matching) with pgvector (semantic vector search). Every result is ACL-filtered per user. Catalog uses lightweight keyword search only. Dynamic+ tiers can opt into Quickwit splits over object storage for very large corpora.

How do I get data into Aeira?

All paid tiers support bulk ingestion via REST API: push CSV, JSON, or YAML documents directly. Enhanced and Federated tiers add high-throughput async ingestion via Kafka. We publish SDKs, CLI tools, and example scripts to help you build ingestion pipelines for your data sources.
